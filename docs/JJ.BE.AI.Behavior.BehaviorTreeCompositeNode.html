Class: BehaviorTreeCompositeNode

JJ.BE.AI.Behavior.BehaviorTreeCompositeNode

BehaviorTreeCompositeNode is the base for all of the composite nodes in the behavior tree. Composite nodes have no restrictions on the maximum number of children, but should have at least one child. Nodes with no children should instead be "action" nodes.
